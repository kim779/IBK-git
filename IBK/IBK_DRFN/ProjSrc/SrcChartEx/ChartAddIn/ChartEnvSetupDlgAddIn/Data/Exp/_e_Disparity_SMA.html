Disparity SMA
 

1. OVerview

When it becomes more distant from the moving average line,the stock price turns back around again with the moving average some day .
A disparity is digitized like this quality. A disparity is to analyze which degree estrangement between a price and moving average.
 

2. Fomula

(Today's Price/Today's simple moving average) * 100
Typically: 20-days,60-days
 

3. Use

This interpretation is little different by rise situation and depreciation situation.

Shot-term moving average is mainly used 20-days and long-term used 60-days.

At the time of rise situation
if typically 20days and over 106% ,a point of view for selling, if 98% level, a point of view for buying
if typically 60days and over 110% ,a point of view for buying.

At the time of drop situation
if typically 20days and over 102% ,a point of view for selling. if 92% level, a point of view for buying
if typically 60days and over 104% ,a point of view for selling. if 88% level, a point of view for buying
