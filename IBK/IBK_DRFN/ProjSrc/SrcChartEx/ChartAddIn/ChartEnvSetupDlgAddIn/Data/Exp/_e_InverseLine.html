Counterclockwise direction curve
 

1. Overview

connects intersection of 20-periods moving average of stock price in a vertical axis and 20-periods moving average of volume in a horizontal axis.
It is developed by viewpoint of two theories that the interrelation of stock price and volume is high very and the volume precedes to stock price.It is useful very in stock price mid-term prediction of stock price.
 

2. Fomula

n-periods moving average of price(vertical) and n-periods moving average of volume(horizontal)
Typically n : 20
 

3. Use

- When the volume increases and the stock price's level is bottom,turnround signal from downtrend to uptrend.
- When the volume morely increses and price rises,indicates buy signal.
- When the volume not increases compared to above but the stock price continuously is ascending,continuously buys.
- When the volume decreases more but the stock price does not rise compared to above, From rise it is converted with depreciation.
- When the volume decreases and also the stock price depreciates, becomes sell signal.
