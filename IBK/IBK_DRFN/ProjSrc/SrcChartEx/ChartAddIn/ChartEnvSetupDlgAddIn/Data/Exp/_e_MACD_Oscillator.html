MACD Oscillator
(Moving Average Convergence and Divergence Oscillator)
 

1. Overview

To recognize the intersection of MACD and Signal,MACD Oscillator express the value deducted signal value from MACD value with histogram grape.

 

2. Fomula

MACD-Signal curve
Typically: short-term:12-days, long-term:26-days, Signal:9-days
 

3. Use

When the valuse of MACD Oscillator upward break through zero line,a point of time for buying. When the valuse of MACD Oscillator downward break through zero line,a point of time for selling.
You can analyze a situation by using Divergence.
When the Divergence occurs, the tendency of present time being finished, it will be able to forecast with the fact that the stock price will move with at once new direction. When the stock price is in the process of new low price and MACD Oscillator fails doing in previously highest price breakthrough,you can predict bearish conversion.
When the stock price continuously updates new low price and MACD Oscillator raises and low point little by little raises simultaneously,you can predict bullish conversion.
