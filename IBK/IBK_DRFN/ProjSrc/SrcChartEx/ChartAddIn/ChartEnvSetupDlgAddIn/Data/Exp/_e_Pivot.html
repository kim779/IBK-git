PIVOT: Channel indicator
 

1. Overview

It is mainly used in the day-trading such as futures trading and is indicator for grasping support and resistance level in short-term.

 

2. Fomula

2nd resistance = Pivot + High - Low
1st resistance = (Pivot * 2) - Low
Pivot Point = (High + Low + CLose)/3
1st Support = (Pivot * 2) - High
2nd Support = Pivot - High + Low

 

3. Use

Because Pivot-point indicates price's average flow in one day,it becomes price's center in short-term.
If the stock price of present time is moving from above Pivot point,bullish.
If the stock price of present time is moving from below Pivot point,bearlish.
If the market is downward trend,the probability where today's low or close decided in 1st or 2st Support level is high. You can predict tomorrow's high or low level by using Pivot analysis.
