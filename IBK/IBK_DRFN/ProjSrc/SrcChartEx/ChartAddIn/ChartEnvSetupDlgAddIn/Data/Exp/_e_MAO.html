MAO (Moving Average Oscillator)
        : trend indicator
 

1. Overview

MAO is indicates the difference between shorter moving average and longer moving average. for decision of trendency.
 

2. Fomula

shorter simple/expponential moving average - longer simple/exponential moving average
 

3. Use

Sell when the oscillator crosses the zero line from above to below. Buy when the oscillator crosses from below to above. Some traders buy the valleys and sell the peaks of the oscillator.
