BWI
 

1. Overview

This is the index which shows time change of price change(Volatility Change).

When grasping a price volatility,if price is high it is general that volatility is big and if price is low it is general that volatility is low.
Consequently it is measure adjusted volatility by price width of Bollingerband(Upper band - Lower band) divide by central line of stock price(means tendency) .
 

2 .Use

The low value of BWI relativy is that the width of Bollingerband becames narrow. Consequently it interprets that the possibility of tendency change in the future is big.
