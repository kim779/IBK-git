William's %R
 

1. Overview

An oscillator that indicates overbought and oversold states.it has meaning which is simiar with Stochastics.
 

2. Formula

[CLOSEn-(HIGHn)/(HIGHn -LOWn)] * 100
HIGHn is the highest price in the last n intervals.
CLOSEn is last close of the interval.
LOWn is the lowest price in the interval
 

3. Use

The range of the indicator is 0 to -100.If the indicator is between 0 and -20 this indicates a SELL. If the indicator is between -80 and -100 this indicates a BUY.You must do buying or selling after confirming trendency through signal occurs.
