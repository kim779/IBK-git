Band %b
 

1. Overview

Band % b is a oscillator index and is calculated by application with Bollinger Bands.

 

2. Fomula

(Close - value of Bollingerband lower band) / (value of Bollingerband upper- value of Bollingerband lower band) * 100
 

3. Use

Band % b is a index to conform an overheating or stagnation situationof of price and is used with Bollinger Bands together.
When the value of Band % b is 100,price means being in upper band. When the value of Band % b is 0,price means being in upper band.
You can analyze the situation with Divergence.
When the Divergence occurs,the tendency of present time being finished, it will be able to forecast with the fact that the stock price will move with at once new direction.
When the stock price is in the update of new high price and Band % b fails doing in previously highest price breakthrough,you can predict bearish conversion. When the stock price continuously updates new low price and Band % b raises and low point little by little raises simultaneously,you can predict bullish conversion.
