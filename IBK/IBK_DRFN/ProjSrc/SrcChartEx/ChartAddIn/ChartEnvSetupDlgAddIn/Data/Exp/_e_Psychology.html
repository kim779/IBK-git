Investment psychological line
 

1. Overview

we can grasps which it probably is a stagnation situation or overheating situation in equity market by investment psychological line and decide a short-term selling and buying point of view.
 

2. Fomula

(if (Today's close - Yesterday's,accumulate by 1 in n-periods) / n) * 100
 

3. Use

If indicator value =< 30%,a point of time for buying
If indicator value >= 70%,a point of time for selling.
