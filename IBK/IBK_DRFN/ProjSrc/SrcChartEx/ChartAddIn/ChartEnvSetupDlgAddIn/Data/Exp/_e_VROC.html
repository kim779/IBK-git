Volume ROC (Volume Rate Of Change)
 

1. Overview

The Volume Rate-of-Change ("ROC") is calculated identically to the Price ROC, except it displays the ROC of the security's volume, rather than of its closing price.

 

2. Fomula

(Today Volume - Volume n-periods ago)/ Volume n-periods ago * 100
Basic setting value: 12day
 

3. Use

RAlmost every significant chart formation (e.g., tops, bottoms, breakouts, etc) is accompanied by a sharp increase in volume. The Volume ROC shows the speed at which volume is changing.
