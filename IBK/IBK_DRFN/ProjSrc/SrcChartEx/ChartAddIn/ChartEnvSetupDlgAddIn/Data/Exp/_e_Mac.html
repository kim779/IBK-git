MAC(Moving Average Channels)
 

1. Overview

MAC display moving averages of high and low on a chart and compose of 4 lines.
 

2. Fomula

first line : high among moving average of 5-periods X 1.1
second line : high among moving average of 5-periods
third line : low among moving average of 5-periods
fourth : low among moving average of 5-periods X 0.9
 

3. Use

use band
(1) If the stock price doesn't upwardly break through the first line and again depreciates,a point of time for selling time
(2) If the stock price upwardly breaks through the first line,a point of time for buying
(3) After the stock price upwardly breaks through the first line,if it again falls with a 1st line lower part,a point of time for selling time
(4) If the stock price downwardly breaks through the fourth line,a point of time for selling time
(5) After the stock price downwardly break through the fourth line,if it again upwardly breaks through the line,a point of time for buying
(6) If the stock price doesn't downwardly break through the fourth line and again upwardly rises,a point of time for buying
