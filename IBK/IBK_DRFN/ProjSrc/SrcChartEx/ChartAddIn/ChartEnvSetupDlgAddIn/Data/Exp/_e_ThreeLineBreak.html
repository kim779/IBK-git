ThreeLineBreak
 

1. Overview

Three Line Break charts display a series of vertical boxes ("lines") that are based on changes in prices. As with Kagi, Point & Figure, and Renko charts, Three Line Break charts ignore the passage of time.
 

2. Fomula

1. If the price exceeds the previous line's high price, a new white line is drawn.
2. If the price falls below the previous line's low price, a new black line is drawn.
3. If the price does not rise above nor fall below the previous line, nothing is drawn
 

3. Use
1. Buy when a white line emerges after three adjacent black lines (a "white turnaround line").
2. Sell when a black line appears after three adjacent white lines (a "black turnaround line").
3. Avoid trading in "trendless" markets where the lines alternate between black and white
