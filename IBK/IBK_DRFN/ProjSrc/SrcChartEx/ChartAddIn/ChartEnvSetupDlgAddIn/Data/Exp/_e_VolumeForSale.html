Histogram of the potential selling accumulated volume
 

1. Overview

It shows the ratio of volume accumulated by price by histogram on price ranges.

 

2. Fomula

It sets price ranges with a identical ratio in vertical axis.
It display horizontal histogram of accumulated volumes by price ranges on price chart.
 

3. Use

If the stock price located above the potential selling accumulated volume, it is role of support block. In the block with small accumulate volume,it is general that the price's movement is rapid.
