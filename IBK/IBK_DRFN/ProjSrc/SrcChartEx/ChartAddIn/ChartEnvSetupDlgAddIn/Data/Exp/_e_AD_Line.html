A/D (Accumulation/Distribution)
 

1. Overview
The Accumulation/Distribution Line was developed by Marc Chaikin to assess the cumulative flow of money into and out of a security.
 

2. Formula

¢²[{(Close-Low)-(High-Close)/(High-Low)} * Volume]
accumulation value from the beginning

cf) It is an accumulation from initial value in the duration referred from the graph screen .

 

3. Use

1.The Accumulation/Distribution Line will help to determine if the volume in a security is increasing on the advances or declines.
2.The Accumulation/Distribution Line can be used to gauge the general flow of money. An uptrend indicates that buying pressure is prevailing, and a downtrend indicates that selling pressure is prevailing.
3.The Accumulation/Distribution Line can be used to spot divergences, both positive and negative.
4.The Accumulation/Distribution Line can be used to confirm the strength and sustainability behind a move.
