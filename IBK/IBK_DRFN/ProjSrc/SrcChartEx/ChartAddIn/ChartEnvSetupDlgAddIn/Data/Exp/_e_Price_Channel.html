Price channel
 

1. Overview

This index means the support and resistance line. The upper line connects the highest of n-periods ago and the lower line connects the lowest of n-periods ago.

 

2. Fomula

Base line connects close
Upper line connects the highest of n-periods.
Lower line connects the lowest of n-periods
 

3. Use

If the price goes toward the upper band,means an overbought,
If the price goes toward the lower band,means an oversold.
