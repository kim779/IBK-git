RMI(Relative Momentum Index)
 

1. Overview

It is devised index to interprete easily by putting the concept of the momentum in RSI index.
 

2. Fomula

RMI = 100 - [100 / {1 + (A / B)}]

A (Rise momentum average)
= Simple moving average n periods of positive momentum

B (Depreciation momentum average)
= Simple moving average n periods of negative momentum
(IF Depreciation momentum average = 0 : RMI = 100)

Momentum = Today's close - close 5 days agorhkao
 

3. Use

Use Overbought/Oversold
If RMI below 30,buying signal because of oversold situation. If RMI over 70,selling signal because of overbought situation.

Use disparity
The stock price reachs new high price but the RMI is previously compared to low-end levellow level, becuase of bearish disparity selling signal. Case of opposition,because of bullish,buying signal
