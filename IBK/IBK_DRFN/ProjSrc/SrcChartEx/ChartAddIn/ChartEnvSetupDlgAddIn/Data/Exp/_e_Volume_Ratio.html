VR (Volume Ratio)
 

1. Meaning

It compares the volume of market price rise day and the volume of market price depreciation day during specific periods(20-days) and shows with percentage. It is market quality index to grasp which the market is overheating or stagnation .
 

2. Fomula

{(sum of the volume of market price rise day+sum of the volume of market price equal day*0.5)/(sum of the volume of market price depreciation day+sum of the volume of market price equal day*0.5)} * 100

Typically : 20-days
 

3. Use

If VR below 70%, Stagnation situation. If 150% <= VR <= 200%,normal If VR over 450%, Overheating situation.
From the rise market the market price continues rising by overheating psychological and the case which it ascends is many. VR at the time of stagnation is reliable relatively.
