AB Ratio
 

1. Overview

After AB ratio grasps strong/weak energy by using a stock price fluctuation and predicts price's movement.

 

2. Fomula

A ratio = (sum of 26-periods strong energy)
/ (sum of 26-periods weak energy26) * 100
Strong energy = Today's high - Today's open
Weak energy = Today's open - Today's low

B ratio = (sum of 26-periods strong energy)
/ (sum of 26-periods weak energy26) * 100
Strong energy = Today's high - yesterday's close
Weak energy = yesterday's close - Today's low
 

3. Use

Use base line
If B ratio upward cross A Ratio,Buying siganl. If B ratio downward cross A Ratio,Selling siganl.

At the time of drop situation
If two lines rise together,Bullish market.
If two lines depreciate together,Bearish market.
