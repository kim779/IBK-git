GlanceBalance
 

1. Overview

Conversion line and base line: If The conversion line is located above base line and the stock price line is located above base line.the base line becomes the support line.
Escorting SPAN: THE Escorting SPAN upward break through the stock price 26days ago,a point of view for buying.In opposition downward break through,a point of view for selling.
Preceding SPAN1,Preceding SPAN2 ==> Cloud BAND: Gap between Preceding SPAN1 and Preceding SPAN1.If the stock price upward break through Cloud BAND a point of view for buying and if downward break through Cloud BAND a point of view for selling. Also it does a support line role in upward trend and a resistance line role in downward trend.
 

2. Fomula

Base Line = (the highest of 26days periods + the lowest of 26days periods) / 2
Conversion line = (the highest of 9days periods + the lowest of 9days periods) / 2
Escorting SPAN = the close of that day does after 26th and draws line
Short-term Preceding SPAN= {the value of (Base Line + Conversion line) / 2} procedes 26th and draws line
Long-term Preceding SPAN = the value of {(the highest of 52days periods + the lowest of 52days periods) / 2} procedes 26th and draws line
 

3. Use

Use the base line
If the conversion line located above the base line,a point of view for buying. If the conversion line located below the base line,a point of view for selling. When grasping a selling and buying point of view in like this base line, through observation the movement of base line,if the base line is upward trend,holding sell. if the base line is downward trend,holding buy.

Use the escorting line
When the base line is maintaining a upward trend and a escorting line upward break through the stock price, the probability which will be converted to bullish market is high. At this time if a escorting line fails completely upward break through the stock price and again reverses with downtrend the probability which will be converted to strong bearish market is high.
If a escorting line downward break through the stock price,a point of view for selling. If a escorting line depreciated fails completely downward break through the stock price and again reverses with uptrend, the probability which will be converted to strong bullish market is high.

Use Cloud BAND
The Cloud BAND does a support line role in upward trend and a resistance line role in downward trend The thickness of clode band is closely related with the strength of supprt and resistance.
