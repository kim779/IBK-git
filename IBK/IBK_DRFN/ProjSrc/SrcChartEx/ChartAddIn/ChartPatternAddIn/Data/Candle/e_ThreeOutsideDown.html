[ThreeOutsideDown]Bearish
The Bearish Three Outside Down Pattern is another name for the Confirmed Bearish Engulfing Pattern. The third day confirms the bearish trend reversal.The first two days forms a Bearish Engulfing Pattern, and the third day confirms the reversal suggested by the Bearish Engulfing Pattern since it is a black candlestick closing with a new low for the three days.
