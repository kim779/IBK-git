DAYOFWEEK The function calculates a day of the week of the date when it designates.

DAYOFWEEK (
  nDate
);

Parameter nDate
[nDate] Input the date with the year month and day. 2005 December 1st inputs with 20051201

Output
[Output] It outputs the day of the week of nDate. Sunday = 0, Monday = 1, Tuesday = 2,.. Saturday = 6

Examples
VARIABLE : nDate(0);
nDate = DAYOFWEEK(20050916);
// 2005 September 16th is Friday and the nData is 5.
